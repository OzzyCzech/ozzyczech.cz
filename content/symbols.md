<div class="text-4xl leading-7">

`✓` - Tick
`` - Apple Logo
`⌘` - Command (or Cmd)
`⇧` - Shift
`⌥` - Option (or Alt)
`⌃` - Control (or Ctrl)
`✗` - cross `&#x2717;`
`✘` - cross `&#x2718;`
`°` - Degree Sign
`✕` `&#x2715;` and `✓` `&#x2713;`
`✖` `&#x2716;` and `✔` `&#x2714;`
`×` `&#x00d7;` `&times;`
`…` - Horizontal Ellipsis `&hellip;`

</div>
