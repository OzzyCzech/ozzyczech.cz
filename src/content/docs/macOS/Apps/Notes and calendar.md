Apps for capturing notes, keeping a journal, and managing calendar events on macOS.

## Note-taking

- **[Bear](https://bear.app/)** — Markdown note-taking app with a polished editor, tags, and iCloud sync for Mac and iOS
- **[Heynote](https://heynote.com/)** — persistent scratch pad for developers; supports multiple blocks with per-block language formatting
- **[Notion](https://www.notion.so/)** — all-in-one workspace combining notes, databases, wikis, and project tracking

## Journaling

- **[Linked](https://uselinked.com/)** — [open-source](https://github.com/lostdesign/linked) distraction-free daily journaling app for macOS

## Calendar

- **[Czech Holidays Calendar](webcal://www.google.com/calendar/ical/czech__cs%40holiday.calendar.google.com/public/basic.ics)** — Google Calendar feed for Czech public holidays (webcal subscription link)
