## Z-mount lenses

**Nikon Z mount** covers both **FX (full frame)** and **DX (APS-C)** mirrorless bodies.  
On DX bodies, the field of view is approximately **1.5×** tighter than full frame.

**Resources:** [NIKKOR Z lens lineup](https://www.nikon.cz/cs_CZ/products/category_pages/lenses.page) · [Nikon](https://www.nikon.cz/) · [Nikon cameras](/photography/nikon/)

### S-line and name suffixes

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **S-line** | Higher-end optical/mechanical line within Z mount. |
| **VR** | Vibration Reduction (optical stabilization). |
| **MC** | Macro lens design. |
| **DX** | APS-C image circle for Nikon DX mirrorless bodies. |
