## RF-S lenses

**RF-S** lenses are native APS-C optics for EOS R7 / R10 / R50 / R50 V. They use the same **RF** mount as full-frame glass.

**Full-frame RF** lenses (wider image circle, often heavier): **[RF lenses](/photography/canon/rf-lenses/)** — including L-series zooms, macro, teleconverters, and long telephoto options that work on APS-C with ~**1.6×** crop (e.g. 50 mm → ~80 mm equivalent).

Naming (**L**, **IS**, **STM**, **USM**, **Macro**) matches the RF line — see **[suffix legend](/photography/canon/rf-lenses/#lens-name-suffixes)** on the RF page.

### Macro, teleconverters, and longer reach

There are no dedicated **RF-S** macro primes or RF-S teleconverters. Use **RF** lenses from **[RF lenses](/photography/canon/rf-lenses/)** (macro section, teleconverters, and telephoto zooms).
