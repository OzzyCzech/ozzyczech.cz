---
title: Canon lenses
---

Canon EOS R system uses the **RF** mount. Glass splits into full-frame **RF** and APS-C native **RF-S** lines.

- **[RF lenses](/photography/canon/rf-lenses/)** — full-frame image circle; usable on every EOS R body (on APS-C you get ~**1.6×** crop).
- **[RF-S lenses](/photography/canon/rf-s-lenses/)** — smaller, lighter native APS-C options for R7 / R10 / R50 family.

**Resources:** [Canon RF / RF-S lens catalog](https://www.canon.cz/lenses/) · [Canon](/photography/canon/cameras/) camera bodies
