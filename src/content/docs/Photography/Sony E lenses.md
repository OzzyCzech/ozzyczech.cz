## E lenses (APS-C)

**E** lenses here are **APS-C–native** (no **FE** full-frame coverage). On **full-frame** bodies they usually **vignette** unless you use **APS-C crop** mode. For full-frame glass, see [Sony FE lenses](/photography/sony-fe-lenses/).

**E mount** is shared with all mirrorless **Alpha** / **ZV** bodies. **A-mount** (DSLR) lenses need a **LA-EA** adapter; AF may be limited.

- [E-mount lens catalog](https://www.sony.com/imaging/c/lenses)
- [Lens / body compatibility](https://support.d-imaging.sony.co.jp/www/cscs/lens_body/)

### G Master, G, ZEISS, and name suffixes

Same naming as on full-frame; **FE** means full-frame circle (see [Sony FE lenses](/photography/sony-fe-lenses/#g-master-g-zeiss-and-name-suffixes)).

| Badge | Role |
|-------|------|
| **G Master (GM)** | Flagship: resolution, bokeh, AF; often sealed. |
| **G** | Strong performance, often smaller than GM. |
| **ZEISS** | Older **ZA** co-brand; mostly used market now. |

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **FE** | Full-frame image circle (lens may still be used on **APS-C**). |
| **OSS** | In-lens stabilisation. |
| **PZ** | Power zoom (video). |
| **SSM / SAM** | Older AF drive types. |
| **XD / Linear** | Fast, quiet AF on newer GM/G. |

**Firmware:** camera menus or [support](https://www.sony.com/electronics/support) by **SEL…** model.
