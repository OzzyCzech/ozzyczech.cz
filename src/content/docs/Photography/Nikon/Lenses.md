## Z-mount lenses (FX + DX)

**Nikon Z mount** is shared across full-frame (**FX**) and APS-C (**DX**) mirrorless bodies.  
On **DX** cameras, full-frame lenses give a **1.5×** tighter field of view.

### S-line, VR, MC, and suffixes

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **S** | Premium optical/mechanical line for Z mount. |
| **VR** | Vibration Reduction (optical image stabilisation). |
| **MC** | Micro (macro) lens. |
| **DX** | APS-C image circle. |
| **SE** | Special Edition retro styling (e.g. selected primes). |
| **TC** | Teleconverter accessory for compatible tele lenses. |

**F-mount compatibility:** older DSLR lenses can be adapted via **FTZ / FTZ II**, but size, AF speed, and full feature support vary by lens.

**Firmware:** keep body and lens firmware updated via Nikon support pages and camera update workflow.
