- **[Midjourney](https://www.midjourney.com/)** Widely considered the gold standard for artistic and high-fidelity AI imagery.
- **[Flux](https://blackforestlabs.ai/)** Open-weights powerhouse from Black Forest Labs, famous for perfect text rendering and photorealism.
- **[Adobe Firefly](https://firefly.adobe.com/)** Commercially safe AI models integrated directly into Photoshop and Illustrator.
- **[Luma Dream Machine](https://lumalabs.ai/dream-machine)** High-fidelity video generator capable of creating realistic 5-second clips from text and images.
- **[Sora / Veo](https://veo.google/)** State-of-the-art cinematic video generation tools from OpenAI and Google.
- **[NightCafe](https://nightcafe.studio/)** AI-powered image and video generator.
- **[Mystic by Freepik](https://www.freepik.com/ai/image-generator)** AI-powered image generator.
