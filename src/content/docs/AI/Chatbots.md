The main general-purpose AI chatbots available as web interfaces.

**[ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com/)** — conversational AI by OpenAI, one of the most widely used LLM interfaces.

**[Claude](https://claude.ai/)** — conversational AI by Anthropic, focused on safety and long-context tasks.

**[Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/)** — Google's conversational AI, integrated with Google Workspace and Search.

**[Perplexity](https://www.perplexity.ai/)** — AI-powered search engine that answers questions with cited sources in real time.
